Hurricane Facts

  • Hurricanes are classified into five categories based on wind speed.
  • The strongest hurricane recorded was Hurricane Patricia in 2015.
  • Hurricanes form over warm ocean waters and lose strength over land.
  • The hurricane season in the Atlantic runs from June to November.
